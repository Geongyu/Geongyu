## 🔬 Research Focus

My research centers on translating **visual phenotypes into molecular and clinical insights**:

- Pathology Foundation Models (WSI, MIL, knowledge distillation)
- Multi-Omics & Proteomics Integration with Histopathology
- Virtual Cell Modeling & Cell Perturbation Prediction
- Drug Response Prediction & ADMET Modeling
- Clinical Decision Support Systems (CDSS) for Oncology
- Calibration, Uncertainty Quantification & Model Interpretability
